Zinger Burger

Description

Satisfy your craving for bold and zesty flavors with our irresistible Zinger Burger. A perfect fusion of crispy and succulent, this burger is a feast for the senses. The golden, spicy chicken patty is layered with fresh lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and a special zesty sauce, all encased in a soft and toasted bun. Whether you're seeking a quick and delicious meal or looking to spice up your gatherings, our Zinger Burger promises a delightful combination of crunch and spice that will leave you coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast fillets
  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Buns (preferably brioche)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Hot sauce
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken breast fillets into burger-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, marinate the chicken in buttermilk for at least 30 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornstarch, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to create the coating for the chicken.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or skillet to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Dredge the marinated chicken in the flour mixture, ensuring each piece is well-coated.
  6. Fry the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Mix mayonnaise with hot sauce to create a zesty sauce.
  8. Toast the buns in the oven or on a skillet until golden.
  9. Assemble the Zinger Burger: Spread the zesty sauce on the bottom bun, add a piece of crispy chicken, and top with lettuce and tomato slices. Add pickles if desired.
  10. Place the top bun on the stack and press gently to create the perfect Zinger Burger.
  11. Serve immediately and relish the bold flavors of this crispy and spicy delight. Enjoy your homemade Zinger Burger experience!
